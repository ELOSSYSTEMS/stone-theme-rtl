# Clean Canvas — support terms (what they will/won’t do)

## Support coverage (Verified)
- They will explain how themes work to help you build a store.
- Free support is limited to the theme “as originally provided.”
- Custom features, coding, apps are not covered.  
Source: https://support.cleancanvas.co.uk/hc/en-us/articles/12067015902237-Support-terms citeturn3search6

## Hard limits that matter for STONE (Verified)
- No support for troubleshooting bugs caused by apps and custom code.
- No support for very old versions / pre-OS2.
- Support limits for older than a threshold from latest version (see source).  
Source: https://support.cleancanvas.co.uk/hc/en-us/articles/12067015902237-Support-terms citeturn3search6

## Extraction (Inference)
Your theme must be:
- OS2-first
- upgradeable
- capable via settings (to avoid “custom coding” requests)
