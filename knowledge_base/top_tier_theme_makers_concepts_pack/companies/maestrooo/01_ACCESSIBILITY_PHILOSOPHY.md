# Maestrooo — accessibility philosophy

## What they state (Verified)
- They value accessibility and follow best practices.
- They do not guarantee full ADA compliance; compliance is complex.
- They recommend limiting apps because apps might add inaccessible code.  
Source: https://support.maestrooo.com/article/686-accessibility-ada-a11y citeturn1search3

## Extraction (Inference)
- A theme can be “a11y-aware,” but merchant content + apps can break it.
- For STONE: require a11y checks at section level and define “app boundaries.”
