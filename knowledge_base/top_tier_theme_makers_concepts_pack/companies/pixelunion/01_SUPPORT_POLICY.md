# Pixel Union — support boundaries (public)

## What they support (Verified)
- Guidance on theme features, functionality, and configuring settings.
- Shopify compatibility issues arising from a standard theme install.
- Bug reports due to the theme in its original state.  
Source: https://support.pixelunion.net/hc/en-us/articles/360022344033-Pixel-Union-Support-Policy citeturn2search4

## What they don’t support (Verified signals)
- Shopify admin “how-to” questions.
- Custom feature development or custom designs (they can refer you to experts).  
Source: https://support.pixelunion.net/hc/en-us/articles/360022344033-Pixel-Union-Support-Policy citeturn2search4

## Extraction (Inference)
Build your theme so “most requests” are solvable through:
- settings and blocks
- template variants
- documented workflows
