# Pixel Union — Theme Updater doctrine

Pixel Union documents that:
- Content/settings/customization transfer is a Pro feature.
- The app can surface conflicts requiring manual adjustments.
- It provides a “conflicted customizations” comparison flow.  
Source: https://support.pixelunion.net/hc/en-us/articles/7141845808147-How-to-update-with-Theme-Updater citeturn3search3

They also present “best way to update” decision flows that emphasize budget + customization degree.  
Source: https://support.pixelunion.net/hc/en-us/articles/5706150519315-What-s-the-best-way-for-me-to-update-my-theme citeturn3search5

## Extraction (Inference)
- Updating is a **business decision**: time vs cost vs risk.
- Your theme should minimize “conflict surface” by isolating custom code.
