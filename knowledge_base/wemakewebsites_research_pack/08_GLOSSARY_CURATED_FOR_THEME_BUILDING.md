# WMW Glossary — Curated Terms for Theme Building

Source glossary hub: https://www.wemakewebsites.com/glossary

Goal: Provide a shortlist of terms that matter when building and QA’ing a Shopify theme.
Note: This is a curated list; the full glossary is much larger.

## Core terms to align with your theme build
- Accessibility — ensuring sites are usable by people with disabilities; WMW references WCAG AA on their accessibility page.
  Sources:
  - https://www.wemakewebsites.com/glossary
  - https://www.wemakewebsites.com/accessibility
- CRO (Conversion Rate Optimization) — optimizing flows to increase conversion.
  Source: https://www.wemakewebsites.com/glossary
- A/B Testing — split testing variations.
  Source: https://www.wemakewebsites.com/glossary
- Bounce Rate — % of single-page sessions.
  Source: https://www.wemakewebsites.com/glossary
- AOV (Average Order Value) — average spend per order.
  Source: https://www.wemakewebsites.com/glossary
- CAC / CLV — acquisition cost and lifetime value (useful for prioritizing work).
  Source: https://www.wemakewebsites.com/glossary

## Theme-specific engineering vocabulary (add to your IDE’s language)
- Inference: CLS / LCP / INP — Core Web Vitals vocabulary you should enforce in QA.
- Inference: “LTR islands” — components that must remain LTR even in RTL (e.g., credit card numbers, SKUs).
- Inference: “Directional icons” — arrows/chevrons that must mirror in RTL.
